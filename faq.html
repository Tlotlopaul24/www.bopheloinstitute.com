Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What programs does Bophelo Institute offer?

We offer accredited NATED programs from N4 to N6 in various fields such as Human Resource Management, Financial Management, Electrical Engineering, Farming Management, and more.

2. How do I apply?

Click on the “Apply Now” link in the menu and complete the online application form. You’ll also need to email a copy of your ID and academic results to bopheloinstitute1@gmail.com.

3. What are the minimum entry requirements?

You need at least a National Senior Certificate (Matric), NCV Level 4, or equivalent qualification. Some programs may accept Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) for mature applicants.

4. Is the college accredited?

Yes, Bophelo Institute is accredited to offer National N Diploma qualifications as approved by the Department of Higher Education and Training (DHET).

5. Do you offer distance learning or part-time study?

Currently, we offer full-time contact learning. Plans for part-time or hybrid study options may be introduced in the future.

6. How long does each program take?

Each level (N4, N5, N6) takes 6 months. After completing all 3 levels (18 months), you will complete an additional 18 months of workplace training for the National Diploma.

7. Do you help students with internships?

Yes. Bophelo Institute offers internship placement support, logbook guidance, and readiness workshops to help students gain in-service training experience.

8. What support services are available to students?

We offer CV writing help, interview preparation, career guidance, and soft skills training through workshops and student support staff.

9. How do I contact the college for more information?

You can visit our Contact Us page or email us directly at bopheloinstitute1@gmail.com.
