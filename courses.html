Public Management (N4–N6) Career Pathway

Entry Requirements

  • National Senior Certificate (NSC)/Grade 12
  • NCV Level 4 in a related field
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) for mature students

Subjects in Public Management N4

Subject Description
Public Administration N4 Basic functions of government and public institutions; service delivery
Municipal Administration N4 Functions of municipalities, local governance, service provision
Management Communication N4 Effective workplace communication (written/oral) in public sector contexts
Computer Practice N4 Basic computer literacy, MS Office tools, public service computing basics

Subjects in Public Management N5

Subject Description
Public Administration N5 Public policy, departmental organization, public service ethics
Municipal Administration N5 Local government frameworks, community services, municipal finance
Public Finance N5 Government budgeting, financial planning, procurement processes
Computer Practice N5 Intermediate computer skills, MS Excel, Word, PowerPoint

Subjects in Public Management N6

Subject Description
Public Administration N6 Advanced public policy, government structures, service delivery
Municipal Administration N6 Urban governance, IDPs, municipal budgeting
Public Finance N6 Government accounting, auditing, procurement law
Computer Practice N6 Advanced computing, data processing, reporting

Career Opportunities After N6

  • Public Administrator
  • Municipal Officer
  • Policy Assistant
  • Finance/Admin Officer
  • Procurement Clerk
  • Project Coordinator

How to Get the National N Diploma

  1. Complete N4, N5, and N6 theory (classroom)
  2. Undergo 18 months of internship in public administration
  3. Submit your practical training logbook
  4. Apply for the National N Diploma

Further Education & Vertical Articulation

  • Advanced Diploma or Bachelor's in:
    • Public Management
    • Municipal Governance
    • Development Studies
  • Apply for public internships or bursaries
  • Professional development through SETAs or QCTO

Skills & Knowledge Gained

  • Public policy and administration
  • Governance and accountability
  • Public financial management
  • Project management
  • Leadership and management

Financial Management (N4–N6) Career Pathway

NATED refers to the National Accredited Technical Education Diploma programs in South Africa, designed to provide practical skills in specific fields like financial management.

Entry Requirements

Grade 12 with Accounting and Maths literacy or Pure Maths.

Program Structure: 18 months academic + 18 months work-integrated learning.

N4 Financial Management

Entry Requirements:

  • Grade 12 (NSC) or equivalent qualification
  • Proficiency in Mathematics and English recommended
  • Mature students (23+) with work experience may apply under RPL

Subjects

Subject Name Description
Financial Accounting N4 Introductory accounting concepts, double-entry bookkeeping, balance sheets, ledgers.
Computerised Financial Systems N4 Practical training in accounting software like Pastel or Sage.
Management Communication N4 Business writing, professional communication, meetings, and reporting skills.
Entrepreneurship & Business Management N4 Fundamentals of starting, managing, and growing a business.

N5 Financial Management

Entry Requirements:

  • Successful completion of Financial Management N4
  • OR equivalent qualification in Financial or Business Management
  • RPL may be considered for experienced applicants

Subjects

Subject Name Description
Financial Accounting N5 Intermediate accounting: final accounts, cash flows, analysis of financial statements.
Computerised Financial Systems N5 Advanced usage of accounting packages like Sage, Pastel.
Cost and Management Accounting N5 Basics of costing, budgeting, cost control, break-even analysis.
Entrepreneurship & Business Management N5 Strategic planning, business functions, and decision-making for entrepreneurs.

N6 Financial Management

Entry Requirements:

  • Successful completion of Financial Management N5
  • OR an equivalent Financial/Accounting qualification at N5 level
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) for experienced candidates

Subjects

Subject Name Description
Financial Accounting N6 Advanced accounting: company accounts, consolidated financials, ratio analysis.
Cost and Management Accounting N6 In-depth study of cost centers, budgeting, overhead analysis, marginal costing.
Computerised Financial Systems N6 Proficient use of financial software for full accounting cycles and reporting.
Income Tax N6 South African tax law, tax calculations for individuals and businesses, VAT.

After Completing N6

  • Learners must complete 18 months of relevant practical experience (internship or workplace-based training)
  • Submit logbook/workplace evidence to DHET/QCTO
  • Qualify for the National Diploma: Financial Management

Career Opportunities

  • Financial Manager
  • Cost Accountant
  • Payroll Officer
  • Tax Practitioner
  • Accounting Technician
  • Finance Administrator

Further Education and Training

  • National Diploma in Financial Management or related field
  • Bachelor's Degree in Financial Management, Accounting, or related field

Articulation

  • Vertical: BCom in Accounting/Finance
  • Horizontal: Business Administration

Farm Management (N4–N6) Career Pathway

The NATED program in Farm Management equips students with essential business management skills for success in agriculture.

Entry Requirements

  • National Senior Certificate (Grade 12)
  • NCV Level 4 in Agriculture or Business-related field
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) for mature candidates

Program Structure

  • Theoretical Component: 18 months (N4, N5, N6; 6 months each)
  • Practical Component: 18 months in-service training

Subjects in Farm Management N4

Subject Description
Farm Management N4 Introduction to farm business planning, organization, and production systems
Animal Production N4 Livestock production systems, animal health, and feeding strategies
Plant Production N4 Soil management, crop cycles, fertilization, pest control
Agricultural Economics N4 Basics of agricultural markets, pricing, and financial management

Subjects in Farm Management N5

Entry Requirements: Completion of N4 Certificate in Farming Management

Subject Description
Farm Management N5 Advanced planning, business structures, cost management
Animal Production N5 Breeding, disease control, genetics
Plant Production N5 Advanced crop production, irrigation, sustainable techniques
Agricultural Economics N5 Credit management, insurance, market dynamics, risk analysis

Subjects in Farm Management N6

Entry Requirements: Completion of N5 Certificate in Farming Management

Subject Description
Farm Management N6 Strategic planning, agribusiness, records, decision-making
Animal Production N6 Commercial animal production, product quality control, productivity
Plant Production N6 Agronomy, crop budgeting, sustainable technologies
Agricultural Economics N6 Export marketing, policy, cooperative systems

Qualification Pathway

  • 18 months theory (6 months per level: N4, N5, N6)
  • 18 months internship / workplace training
  • Apply for National N Diploma after theory and practical completion

Career Opportunities

  • Farm Manager
  • Agricultural Extension Officer
  • Livestock or Crop Production Supervisor
  • Agribusiness Entrepreneur
  • Co-operative Development Advisor
  • Agricultural Sales/Marketing Agent

Further Study & Articulation

  • Advanced Diplomas or Degrees in Agricultural Management, Economics, Agribusiness, or Agricultural Extension
  • Short courses in Agri-Processing, Organic Farming, Agritech
  • QCTO Artisan qualifications (e.g., Animal Production Technician)

Support Services from Bophelo Institute

  • Logbook and Internship Guidance
  • Practical Simulation Support
  • CV & Interview Skills for Agricultural Sector
  • Access to AgriBEE and Government Farming Initiatives

Electrical Engineering (N4–N6) Career Pathway

Completing the NATED program in Electrical Engineering N4 to N6 opens various career opportunities in the electrical field.

Entry Requirements

  • Grade 12 with Maths & Physical Science or N3 equivalent

Program Structure

  • N4, N5, and N6 certificate levels
  • 18 months theoretical training (6 months per level)
  • 18 months practical workplace experience

Subjects in Electrical Engineering N4

Subject Description
Electrotechnics N4 Basic electrical principles, circuit theory, and electrical machinery concepts
Industrial Electronics N4 Components such as transistors, diodes, amplifiers, and digital circuits
Mathematics N4 Engineering mathematics including algebra, trigonometry, and calculus basics
Engineering Science N4 Mechanics, thermodynamics, fluids, and electricity basics

Subjects in Electrical Engineering N5

Subject Description
Electrotechnics N5 Advanced AC and DC circuits, electrical power systems, and motors
Industrial Electronics N5 Power electronics, transducers, sensors, and feedback systems
Mathematics N5 Advanced engineering math including complex numbers, calculus, and matrices
Power Machines N5 Electrical machines: transformers, motors, alternators, generators

Subjects in Electrical Engineering N6

Subject Description
Electrotechnics N6 Advanced circuits, three-phase systems, electrical installations, regulations
Industrial Electronics N6 Solid-state electronics, instrumentation systems, and automation
Mathematics N6 Engineering math with integration, differential equations, applications
Power Machines N6 Advanced electrical machinery, control, power transmission systems

After Completing N6

  • Apply for National N Diploma after 18 months practical workplace training
  • Work as Electrical Technician, Engineering Assistant, Maintenance Technician, Plant Operator, or Trainee Engineer
  • Qualify to write Trade Tests or pursue Professional Registration through ECSA

Diploma Qualification Path

Step Duration
N4 + N5 + N6 ±18 months total
Practical Experience 18 months (in-service training)
Apply for Diploma Issued by DHET

Vertical Articulation & Further Study

  • Advanced Diploma or Degree programs at Universities of Technology (with RPL or admission tests)
  • Further studies in Electrical Engineering (BEng Tech), Renewable Energy Systems, Automation, and Instrumentation
  • Entrepreneurship or artisan trades

Additional Benefits & Services

  • Practical Logbook Support
  • Workplace Placement Guidance
  • Trade Test Readiness Training
  • Access to Toolkits, Labs, and Online Resources

Career Options

  • Electrical Technician
  • Junior Electrical Engineer
  • Field Service Technician
  • Instrumentation and Control Technician
  • Building Automation Technician
  • Entrepreneurship in electrical contracting or consulting

Further Education and Training

  • National Diploma in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering
  • Short Courses & Certifications in renewable energy, industrial automation, building automation
  • Universities of Technology, Artisan development, QCTO occupational qualifications

Industries and Workplaces

  • Electrical engineering and construction
  • Industrial engineering
  • Process control
  • Digital electronic engineering
  • Industrial electronics engineering
  • Power stations (e.g., ESKOM)
  • Telecommunication companies (e.g., MTN, Vodacom, Cell C, TELKOM)

Management Assistant (N4–N6) Career Pathway

Qualification: National N Diploma in Management Assistant
Path: N4 → N5 → N6 + 18-month internship
Duration: ±3 years total (18 months theory + 18 months practical)

Entry Requirements (N4)

  • National Senior Certificate (Matric), or
  • NCV Level 4 Certificate in a related field, or
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

Subjects for N4 Management Assistant

Subject Description
Office Practice N4 Introduction to office roles, admin procedures, mail handling, time management
Communication N4 Verbal and written business communication, customer interaction, professionalism
Information Processing N4 Keyboarding, document formatting, touch typing, business letters/memos
Entrepreneurship & Business Management N4 Basics of starting, managing, and marketing a small business

Subjects for N5 Management Assistant

Subject Description
Office Practice N5 Managing meetings, diary systems, travel arrangements, confidentiality & protocol
Communication N5 Advanced writing (reports, notices, memos), listening skills, conflict resolution
Information Processing N5 Intermediate document formatting: tables, mail merges, reports, agendas
Public Administration N5 Introduction to civil service, government structures, public policy

Subjects for N6 Management Assistant

Subject Description
Office Practice N6 Advanced office management: supervision, delegation, planning & resource use
Communication N6 High-level business writing, minute-taking, internal/external communication
Information Processing N6 Complex document formatting, reports, forms, financial tables, speed typing
Public Relations N6 Branding, reputation management, event coordination, stakeholder communication

After Completing N6

  • Complete 18 months relevant workplace training with logbook signed by supervisor
  • Awarded National N Diploma in Management Assistant by DHET
  • Equivalent to NQF Level 6

Career Opportunities After N6 + Diploma

Position Workplace
Executive Secretary Corporate, Government, NGO
Office Manager Department, Clinic, or School Office
Personal Assistant (PA) Executives, Directors
Administrative Supervisor Front office, Back office, Shared services
Public Relations Assistant Marketing departments, PR firms
Data or Information Officer Records management, Digital offices

Sectors That Employ Management Assistants

  • Government Departments (Local, Provincial, National)
  • Municipalities and Public Entities
  • Corporate Companies (All Industries)
  • NGOs and Non-Profits
  • Schools, Colleges, and Universities
  • Legal, Finance, and Health Sectors
  • Consulting and Private Practices

Vertical Career Progression

With Experience & Further Study Possible Roles
National N Diploma + Work Experience Office Manager / Senior Admin Officer
Bachelor’s Degree (e.g., Public Admin) Admin Supervisor / Policy Admin
Short Courses (e.g., HR, Project Management) HR Admin / Project Assistant
Advanced Diploma in Management Assistant Manager / Team Coordinator

Further Study Options

  • Diploma or Degree in Office Management, Public Administration, HR Management, Business Administration
  • QCTO/SETA-accredited qualifications in Records Management, Project Coordination, Skills Development Facilitation, Supply Chain Admin

Support from Bophelo Institute
